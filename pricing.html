Usage of the Technology

Throughout contemporary tack and field, photo finishes are frequently used. Sprinting events like the 100-meter dash, 110-meter hurdles, 200-meter run, and 400-meter run are where the photo finish is most commonly used. Due to the minute time differences between each location, the 100 meters can only last 9.5 seconds.
